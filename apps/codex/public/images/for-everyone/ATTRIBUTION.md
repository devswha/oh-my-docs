## Imgflip meme templates (`meme_*.jpg`)

These are popular meme images served by Imgflip's public meme template API.
Underlying photographs have their own (mostly unclear / fair-use) rights. For internal
or transformative usage these are common practice; for commercial publication, verify
with the original rights holders.

### `meme_woman_yelling.jpg`
- **Source**: [Imgflip template "Woman Yelling At Cat"](https://imgflip.com/memetemplate/188390779/Woman-Yelling-At-Cat)
- **Origin**: Composite of a 2011 *Real Housewives of Beverly Hills* still (Taylor Armstrong, Kyle Richards, © Bravo / NBCUniversal) and "Smudge the Cat" photo (© Miranda Stillabower / @smudge_lord, 2018).
- **License**: Composite is widely shared as a meme template; underlying images are copyrighted. Not Creative Commons.

### `meme_buy_a_boat.jpg`
- **Source**: [Imgflip template "I Should Buy A Boat Cat"](https://imgflip.com/memetemplate/1367068/I-Should-Buy-A-Boat-Cat)
- **Origin**: 2009 viral image of a cat appearing to read a newspaper. Original photographer not formally documented.
- **License**: Widely shared as a meme; original rights status unclear. Not Creative Commons.

## Other assets (`cat_*.png`, `cat_*.jpeg`)

The `cat_fight.png`, `cat_hmm.png`, `cat_hmm2.png`, `cat_multi.png`, `cat_sad.jpeg`, `cat_sad2.png`
files were brought in from a local working folder and appear to be popular internet meme images.

If unable to verify, replace with CC-licensed equivalents from Wikimedia Commons or regenerate
via `docs-image-maker`.

## When inserting CC BY-SA images into MDX

Include the credit either inline (caption / alt text) or in a centralised credits block on the page:

```mdx
![Yawning cat](/images/for-everyone/wm_yawn.jpg)
*Photo by Uli Herrmann, CC BY-SA 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons*
```
